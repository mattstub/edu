Wolf

Wolves only breed once a year with a litter of 4-6 pups

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus occidentalis
  • Pack Size: 6-12 Wolves
  • Habitat: Rocky Mountains
  • Average Lifespan: 13 years

This subspecies of gray wolf has a coat of black, white, gray, tan and even blue-ish. Gray or black wolves are the most common color phase found to occur. They typically stand about 30 inches at the shoulder and weigh between 85 and 115 pounds, although they can weigh as much as 145 pounds.
