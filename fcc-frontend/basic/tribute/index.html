Shane Smith & The Saints

The Spirit Behind the Music

Shane Smith & The Saints hail out of Austin, Texas. Blending a four-part harmony with hints of aggresive folk, rock and rootsy fiddle filled Americana. Featuring two produced albums, Coast (2015) and Geronimo (2016). This group of guys is bound to leave you asking for more during a live show that you can catch at a small local dive bar throughout the midwest region of the United States.
